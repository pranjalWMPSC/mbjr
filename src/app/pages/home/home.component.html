About

"Main Bhi Jal Rakshak” Campaign (by Water Management & Plumbing Skill Council) was launched on 17th September 2022 in association. The campaign was launched by Hon’ble Union Minister of Education & Skill Development & Entrepreneurship Shri Dharmendra Pradhan, Hon’ble Union Minister of State for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Shri Rajeev Chandrasekhar, in presence of officials from Rotary, SSRDP and WMPSC.
